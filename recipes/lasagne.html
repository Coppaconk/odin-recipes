Lasagne





Description


One hour from start-to-finish (and half of that's in the oven) and you can be serving up a big meaty-cheesy dish of lasagne, with enough for second helpings. Because we all want second helpings when it comes to lasagne right? Serve it with a big salad (I LOVE sliced red onions, crisp lettuce and some crunchy sliced radishes in my side salad) and some buttery garlic bread. Ah. Dinner heaven.


Ingredients


  • onion
  • Olive
  • garlic
  • carrot
  • mushroom
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. Fry all vegetables in a frying pan
  2. Add Beef
  3. Add Tomatoes
  4. Add Pasta Sheets
  5. Bake in Oven for 30 minutes
  6. Take out and allow to rest for 10 minutes
  7. Serve Up and enjoy!
